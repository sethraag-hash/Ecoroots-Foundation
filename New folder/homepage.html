Welcome to Ecoroots Foundation

An NGO founded by Shri. Rakesh Khatri, popularly known as The Nest Man of India, has been diligently pursuing its mission with a profound commitment to Environmental Conservation and Community well-being. Its impact on the society is far-reaching, as it champions various programs designed under the themes of Awareness & Action on Environmental, Ecological or Biodiversity Conservation, Afforestation, Women Empowerment, Rural Development & Livelihood. Headquartered in Delhi but operating nationwide, ERF has not only raised environmental consciousness but has also empowered local communities by providing livelihood opportunities and promoting sustainable practices. A major focus of the foundation is to interact with younger generations, including school and college students, as well as corporate employees, to motivate thought and action towards environmental and ecosystem conservation, considering their influence.

Our Founder

Shri Rakesh Khatri

Nest Man of India

A documentary filmmaker turned environmentalist switched to Environment Conservation full time in 2008 committing himself to it particularly in a pursuit to find a solution to the dwindling population of sparrows, living around human population. After multiple innovations and experimentations, he succeeded in creating a nest out of bamboo, cloth, jute, cotton among other things which have an adoption rate of over 85%. As a result of this, the population of sparrows which was on a steep decline in 2008 is currently at a comfortable figure. In addition to this, he has also been driving multiple innovative projects supported by different corporates and institutions particularly aimed at motivating the thought and action towards Conservational and Community Developmental Activities. Being the founder, he is the force behind the vision and programs of Eco Roots Foundation.
