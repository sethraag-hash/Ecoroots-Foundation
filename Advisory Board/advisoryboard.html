Our Advisory Board

Dr. Rashmi Kant Shukla
(Ex- PCCF, Forest Department, Govt. of Uttar Pradesh)

Originally from Dehradun, he joined the indian forest services in 1981 and has served tirelessly for over 40 years and grew onto becoming the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests, Department of Forest and Environment, Government of Uttar Pradesh.He holds an expertise in Natural Resource Management and Development for nearly 35 years in Himalayan terrain , Terrai Arch, Indo-Gangetic belt and Vindhiyans. He also excels in key Environmental issues as have handled them firsthand and worked with and within the communities to find sustainable solutions to them. He has been studying and popularizing the Biodiversity studies in Hindukush- Kailash Landscape to find sustainable solutions to the local problems faced by the local population.
