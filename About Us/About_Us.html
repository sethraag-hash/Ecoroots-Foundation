OUR MAJOR FOCUS

Eco Roots Foundation is a non-profit organization working for environmental and ecosystem conservation with a focus on interacting with the younger generation to provoke both thought and action towards this. Our major focus is to interact with the younger generation like school and college students along with corporate professionals inciting thought and action towards the environmental and ecosystem conservation.



OUR MAJOR PROGRAM

Bird Houses has been a major program for the organization as Mr. Rakesh Khatri, the founding Trustee of the organization with his passion towards birds and their conservation has been motivating people both young and old towards making and installing bird houses across the country. Hence, he is popularly known as the “Nest Man of India”. Along with this, he is a well-known environmentalist who has been making students across the country aware on the issues of environment.



OUR ACTIVITY

In the past decade since our inception, we have been actively involved with the Youth interacting with children across the country in schools and colleges. Our efforts have been mentioned in the Limca Book of Records - National for conducting theatres on Climate Change across the country in 24 Cities of 17 states in 12 languages engaging with over 1,12,000 children. Before this we got Limca Book of World Record for engaging with 94,000 girls doing theatre on water conservation under our program Neer, Naari aur Vigyaan. We have also been accorded with the prestigious International Green Apple Award in 2012 for making over 1,20,000 handmade Jute nests during workshops with students along with being a part of Earth Day Network Star & IUCN. Our work has also found mention in ICSE BOARD English Course Book in 2019.
